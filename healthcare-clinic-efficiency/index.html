Key Questions

  • What is the average wait time by provider?
  • How many no-shows vs. completed visits each month?
  • Which providers are overbooked relative to appointment duration?
  • Any no-show patterns by age/demographics?

Highlights

  • Average wait times exceeded 25 minutes for key providers.
  • No-shows peaked in late spring, especially ages 18–34.
  • Overbooking correlated with longer waits and missed visits.
  • Recommendations: buffer scheduling, reminders, targeted outreach.
