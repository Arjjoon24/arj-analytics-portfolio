Customer Churn Analysis

Model churn drivers across contract types, tenure, usage, and support complaints. Identify at-risk segments and recommend retention actions.

Key Questions

  • Which plans have the highest churn?
  • How does customer usage correlate with churn?
  • Do complaint volumes predict attrition?

Highlights

  • Month-to-month plans show the highest churn propensity.
  • Very low and very high usage cohorts churn more than mid-usage customers.
  • Spike in complaints precedes churn by 1–2 cycles for many segments.
