Mortgage Client Portfolio Analysis

Overview

Analysis of ~100 mortgage client accounts for payment behavior and risk factors using SQL and visualization dashboards.

Key Insights

  • 20% of accounts contribute to 80% of late payments.
  • Clients with adjustable-rate mortgages show higher default risks.
  • Most high-risk clients are concentrated in two regions.
